JIJUE HEALTH

"HEALTH WITHOUT LIMITS, CARE WITHOUT STIGMA"

Mission: To empower adolescents and young adults in urban slums by providing access to comprehensive Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR) education and services.

Vision: A world where every young person has equitable access to SRHR knowledge and services, free from stigma and barriers.

Services: SRHR education, confidential support, and access to essential products for young people in urban slums.

ABOUT US

Mission Statement: Our mission is to bridge the gap in access to quality SRHR education and services for adolescents and young adults, aged 13-25, living in Kenya's urban slums.

Vision Statement: Our vision is to create a future where all adolescents and young adults, regardless of their socio-economic background, can access the information, tools, and support needed to lead healthy, empowered lives.
